4 Season Pizza

This is a 4 Season Pizza Recipe

A flavorful pizza with a variety of toppings, including mushrooms, artichoke hearts, black olives, and prosciutto. A delicious and satisfying pizza that is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough, made from scratch or store-bought
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped artichoke hearts
  • 1/4 cup black olives, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced prosciutto
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. If using store-bought dough, let it come to room temperature before rolling it out.
  3. Roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  5. Top with the mozzarella cheese, mushrooms, artichoke hearts, black olives, and prosciutto.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
