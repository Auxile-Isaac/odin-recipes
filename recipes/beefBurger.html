Beef Burger

This is a Beef burger Recipe

A juicy, flavorful patty of ground beef, topped with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and your favorite toppings. A classic American comfort food that is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 slices cheese (such as Cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  • Your favorite toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Shape the mixture into 4 equal patties.
  3. Heat a large skillet or grill over medium heat.
  4. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Top each patty with a slice of cheese and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
  6. To assemble the burgers, place a patty on the bottom half of a bun. Top with your favorite toppings and the top half of the bun.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
